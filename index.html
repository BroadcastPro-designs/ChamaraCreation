What I Do

Premium Services

Comprehensive visual production solutions tailored to elevate your brand and capture unforgettable moments — from cinematic storytelling to live broadcast engineering

Videography & Photography

Cinematic shoots, creative movie edits, and comprehensive event documentation. From wedding cinematography to corporate brand films, every project receives the highest production value with meticulous attention to lighting, composition, and color grading.

  • Cinematic Event Coverage (Multi-Camera)
  • Creative Film Editing & Color Grading
  • Brand Story & Corporate Production
  • Aerial Drone Footage Integration
  • Same-Day Edit & Highlight Reels

Live Streaming & Broadcast

Professional multi-camera setups with isolated (ISO) feeds, expert audio mixing, and real-time graphics overlay. We deliver reliable, broadcast-quality live streaming for events of any scale — from intimate ceremonies to large-scale conferences with global audiences.

  • Multi-Camera Live Production (Up to 8 Cameras)
  • ISO Recording & Instant Replay Feeds
  • Professional Audio Mixing & Monitoring
  • Real-time Graphics & Lower Thirds
  • Cloud Streaming to YouTube, Facebook & Custom RTMP

Custom Graphics & Design

Dynamic overlays, custom animated lower thirds, and broadcast-ready graphics that elevate your content. From complete brand identity packages to real-time on-screen visuals, we create professional visual elements that make your production stand out.

  • Animated Lower Thirds & Title Cards
  • Broadcast Overlays & Bug Logos
  • Motion Graphics & Visual Effects
  • Brand Identity & Logo Design
  • Social Media Content Templates
